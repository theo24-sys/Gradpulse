Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Welcome to GradPulse, proudly powered by AKLIN TECHNOLOGIES.

At GradPulse, your privacy and trust are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our platform designed to connect university and TVET students across Kenya with top employers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a tailored, seamless, and effective experience for both students and employers:

  • Personal Identity & Contact Info: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and profile photos.
  • Academic & Professional Data: University/TVET details, courses, grades, resumes, credentials, and work history.
  • Account Activity: Job applications, event RSVPs, private messages, and connection requests.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and usage data to improve our analytics and platform health.

2. How We Use Your Data

GradPulse leverages your data strictly to empower your career journey:

  • To match students with relevant jobs, internships, and networking events.
  • To allow verified employers to review applicant profiles and academic credentials seamlessly.
  • To send important portal notifications, platform updates, and direct messaging between users.
  • To ensure the platform remains safe, secure, and free from fraudulent activity.

3. Data Sharing and Transparency

We believe your data belongs to you. We strictly do not sell your data to third parties. We only share data when essential:

  • With Employers: When a student applies for an opportunity or joins an employer event, the employer gains access to the provided profile details and attached resumes.
  • For Legal Obligations: Only if required by Kenyan law or binding legal processes.
  • With Service Providers: Trusted third-party tools (like email delivery or cloud hosting) bound by strict confidentiality agreements, used simply to keep our platform running.

4. Protecting Your Data

AKLIN TECHNOLOGIES employs modern security standards, including encrypted data transfers (HTTPS), secure password hashing, and active threat monitoring, to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse of your personal data.

5. Your Control & Rights

You remain in full control of your account. You have the right to:

  • Update or correct your profile information at any time.
  • Adjust your privacy settings affecting who can view your profile.
  • Request a full export of your data or permanently delete your account by contacting our support team.

6. AI Data Processing Safeguards

How We Use Artificial Intelligence

GradPulse leverages AI-powered features (including Google Gemini) to enhance your experience. We are committed to transparent and responsible AI use.

  • Purpose-Limited Processing: Your data is processed by AI solely to provide platform features such as transcript parsing, career recommendations, KCSE cluster calculations, and mentor matching. We do not use your data for external AI model training.
  • Third-Party AI Providers: When your data is processed by third-party AI services (e.g., Google Gemini API), it is transmitted securely over encrypted channels and is subject to strict data processing agreements.
  • No Automated Decision-Making: GradPulse does not use AI to make binding decisions about your employment, academic standing, or eligibility. All AI outputs are advisory and should be independently verified.
  • Right to Opt Out: You may choose not to use AI-powered features (e.g., transcript upload, career chatbot) without affecting your access to other Platform features.
  • Data Retention for AI: Data submitted for AI processing is not stored beyond the duration required to deliver the result. Processed outputs (e.g., extracted grades) are stored as part of your account data and subject to your control.

7. Data Breach Response Protocol

Our Commitment If a Breach Occurs

AKLIN TECHNOLOGIES maintains a formal incident response plan in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

  • 72-Hour Notification: In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) within 72 hours of discovery.
  • Breach Assessment: We will immediately investigate the scope, cause, and impact of any breach, and take steps to contain and remediate the incident.
  • User Communication: Affected users will receive clear, plain-language notifications detailing: what data was affected, what we are doing about it, and what steps you can take to protect yourself.
  • Preventive Measures: We employ continuous security monitoring, regular vulnerability assessments, encrypted data storage, secure password hashing (bcrypt), and role-based access controls to minimize breach risk.
  • Employee Training: All AKLIN TECHNOLOGIES personnel with data access undergo regular data protection and security awareness training.
