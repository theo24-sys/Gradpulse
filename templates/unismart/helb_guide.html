HELB & Higher Education Funding

A comprehensive guide on how to secure your university and college funding in Kenya.

What is HEF/HELB?

The Higher Education Financing (HEF) portal is the unified platform for all government financial support for university and college students. This includes Scholarships, Loans (HELB), and Bursaries.

Scholarships

Government grants based on your level of need. You don't pay these back.

HELB Loans

Financial loans that you start repaying after graduation and a grace period.

Bursaries

Additional funds typically provided by constituencies or counties.

How to Apply

1
Registration

Create an account on the HEF portal using your ID/Birth Certificate number and a valid email address.

2
Profile Completion

Fill in your personal, academic, and parents'/guardians' details accurately. You will need your KCSE index number and university admission details.

3
Submit Documents

Upload scanned copies of required documents (ID, Admission Letter, Parent IDs, etc.) and submit.

Tips & Tricks

  • Apply Early: Portals can be slow near deadlines.
  • Correct Phone: Use a Safaricom number registered in your name for M-Pesa disbursements.
  • Guarantors: Have the ID numbers and phone numbers of two non-relative guarantors ready.

Money Management

  • Priority: Use the funds for tuition and accommodation first.
  • Budgeting: Divide the remaining money by the number of months in the semester.
  • Avoid Debt: Don't lend your HELB money to friends; focus on your survival.
